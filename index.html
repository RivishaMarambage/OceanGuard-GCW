Take Action for Our Oceans

Reduce Plastic Pollution

Over 8 million tonnes of plastic enter the ocean every year. Small changes in your daily habits can create a massive impact on reducing ocean plastic waste.

  • Switch to reusable bags, bottles, and straws to cut single-use plastic
  • Participate in local beach and waterway cleanup events
  • Support businesses that use sustainable, plastic-free packaging

Protect Marine Wildlife

Marine species from tiny plankton to great whales are under threat from pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction. We can act to protect them.

  • Report injured or stranded marine animals to local rescue organisations
  • Avoid products made from endangered marine species
  • Support marine protected areas and conservation charities

Sustainable Seafood

Overfishing threatens ocean ecosystems. By making informed seafood choices, you help maintain healthy fish populations and ocean biodiversity.

  • Look for MSC (Marine Stewardship Council) certified seafood labels
  • Reduce seafood consumption and explore plant-based alternatives
  • Ask restaurants about the source and sustainability of their seafood
